GISA Writer's Society

Do you like to write? Are you a poet at heart? Do you have a hard time meeting other writers? Welcome to the GISA Writer's club! Here you can find a safe and quiet space to write with like-minded peers. We cover topics like poetry, storybuilding, getting published, and the finer arts of being an author. We hold a book club every session, and a special time for peer-reviewing your works as well. Meetings occur once every week on Wednesdays in Pflaumer's Room. Email Mailynne Rudnicki for more details.
